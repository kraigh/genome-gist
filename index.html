What is this?

GenomeGist extracts clinically and wellness-relevant genetic variants from raw genome files exported from services like 23andMe. The output is a compact YAML file ready for analysis with AI tools.

  • Upload your raw genome file
  • We match it against a curated list of relevant SNPs
  • Download a summary with your genotypes and annotations

Privacy

Your genetic data never leaves your browser. All processing happens locally on your device. We don't upload, store, or transmit your genome file. The only network requests are for loading the application itself.
